## ğŸ’¡ Tips & Tricks

### For Best Results

1. **Start Broad, Then Narrow**
   - Begin with city or category
   - Add more filters as needed

2. **Use Search for Specific Interests**
   - "sunset", "craft beer", "architecture"
   - Searches across multiple fields

3. **Try Different Price Ranges**
   - Budget: $0-$50
   - Mid-range: $50-$150
   - Premium: $150+

4. **Filter by Language**
   - Essential for communication
   - Guides speak selected language

5. **Check Multiple Categories**
   - Try related categories
   - E.g., "Food" and "Local Life"
